My Hobbies

Hobby 1

I enjoy working on my car and driving when I have the chance to. This is also a great outlet for my photography since car meets and gatherings are a great place to share my photos with people. Although this is an expensive hobby, I trult enjoy being able to go out on a nice summer day with my friends and enjoy the road and the views it brings.

Hobby 2

I've always enjoyed photography, especially capturing cars and cool scenes like this. There's something about finding the right angle, lighting, and composition that makes every shot feel unique. I like experimenting with different perspectives, trying to make a simple moment look more dynamic. Whether it's shooting at events or just messing around with my camera, it's a fun way to be creative and appreciate the details most people might overlook.
